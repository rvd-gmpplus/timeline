Our Vision and Guardrails

Data Vision Statement

"The data of GMP+ International will be the trusted backbone for safe and sustainable feed worldwide.

We will organise and govern our data so that it is reliable, transparent, explainable and secure, with one shared version of the truth for our core business objects.

By using data effectively, we will make the feed supply chain traceable and transparent, enable collaboration across the market, support better-informed decision-making, and continuously improve our internal and external processes - always within clear guardrails for privacy, security, ethics and human oversight."

Our Guardrails

  • Privacy

    Personal and sensitive data handled with care, compliant with privacy legislation.

  • Security

    Data protected with appropriate access controls from the start.

  • Ethics

    Data used in a way that is transparent and ethically sound.

  • Human Oversight

    People remain in control and can understand, question and override outcomes.
